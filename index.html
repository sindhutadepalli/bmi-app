About BMI

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a simple calculation using height and weight. It helps estimate a healthy body weight based on your height.

Categories:
Underweight: BMI < 18.5
Healthy: 18.5 – 24.9
Overweight: 25 – 29.9
Obesity: 30+
